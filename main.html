Spaghetti Recipe


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions


  1. Bring Water to a boil.
  2. Bring Water to a boil.
    1. Add salt to taste
    2. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringng to a simmer
  4. Mix cooked spaghetti with marinara sauce
  5. Enjoy!
